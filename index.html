Importing a Flashcard Set from Quizlet:

To import a flashcard set from Quizlet, you first need to export from Quizlet. In your Quizlet set, click on the three dots and then "export". Under "Between term and definition", click on "custom" and enter "*&". Under "Between rows", click on "custom" and enter "*&". You can then click "Copy text" and paste the copied text into the text box on this page.

Printing your Flashcards

EasyFlashcards makes it simple to print your flashcards on to standard 3" x 5" flashcards. First, click on "Generate Flashcards" to download the two PDFs. One contains the front terms and the other contains the back definitions. Load the notecards in your printer and print the first PDF, with the paper size set to 3" x 5". After those print, repeat with the other PDF, but with the notecards flipped so that it prints on the back. You should now have double-sided notecards!

Importing a Flashcard Set from Quizlet

  • Export from Quizlet:
    • In your Quizlet set, click on the three dots.
    • Select "Export."
    • Under "Between term and definition," click on "custom" and enter "*&"
    • Under "Between rows," click on "custom" and enter "*&"
    • Click "Copy text."
  • Paste into EasyFlashcards:
    • Paste the copied text into the text box on this page.
    • Click on "Generate Flashcards" to create your flashcard set


    • Printing your Flashcards

  • Generate Your Flashcards:
    • Enter your terms into EasyFlashcards or import them from Quizlet
    • Click on "Generate Flashcards."
    • Download the two PDFs that are created: one for the front terms and one for the back definitions.
  • Prepare Your Printer:
    • Load your standard 3" x 5" index cards into your printer.
    • Make sure your printer settings are set to print on 3" x 5" paper.
  • Print the Front Terms:
    • Print the PDF containing the front terms.
  • Print the Back Definitions:
    • Flip the index cards in your printer so that the printed side is facing down.
    • Print the PDF containing the back definitions.

    • You now have double-sided flashcards!
